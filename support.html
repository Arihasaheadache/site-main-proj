Women in Progress

Visualizing the rise of women across education, technology, and leadership

Women in STEM Fields

Women have steadily increased their presence in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), a field historically dominated by men. As education access has grown and workplace barriers are challenged, more women are pursuing STEM degrees and careers.

Women in Leadership Roles

From CEOs to political leaders, the representation of women in leadership roles has significantly grown. This reflects a global shift in recognizing the capabilities and perspectives that women bring to decision-making tables.

Higher Education Enrollment

Globally, women now outnumber men in college and university enrollment in many countries. This is a strong indicator of the next generation’s potential, where women are poised to drive innovation, culture, and governance.
